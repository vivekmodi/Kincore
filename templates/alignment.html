Multiple Sequence Alignment of Protein Kinase Domains

We provide a structure-based multiple sequence alignment of 497 human protein kinase domains. The alignment is parsimonious and arranged in 17 blocks of conserved elements like C-helix, HRD and DFG motifs etc in upper case letters. These are separated by left-justified unaligned sequence containing insertions of different lengths in lower case letters.The list of conserved blocks and corresponding structural elements is displayed in the table and the figure.
